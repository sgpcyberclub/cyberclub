This exclusive club, part of Siliguri Government Polytechnic, is dedicated to the CST department, promoting a positive mindset and continuous learning. Members will participate in weekly webinars focused on real-life projects, programming rules, and regulations.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students by providing the resources, support, and opportunities they need to excel in web and app development. We believe in hands-on learning and real-world application, ensuring our members are well-prepared for the future.

Empowering Future Innovators

Welcome to the Cyber Club, where we are dedicated to enhancing the skills of students in web and app development. Our club provides a collaborative environment for students to learn, grow, and innovate.

What We Do

Skill Development: Through workshops, seminars, and mentorship, we help students develop essential skills in web and app development.

Project-Based Learning: Our members work on a variety of projects, gaining practical experience and building impressive portfolios.

Competitions and Events: We organize and participate in hackathons, coding competitions, and tech events to challenge our members and showcase their talents.
